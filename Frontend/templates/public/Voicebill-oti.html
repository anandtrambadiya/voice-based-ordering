FAQ

Common questions

Do I really own the code with no recurring fees?
Yes, completely. After the one-time payment the integrated code is yours with full ownership. There are no licensing fees, no subscriptions, and no revenue sharing. The only optional ongoing cost is if you choose to use the Claude AI voice parsing API, which has its own free tier covering most small restaurants.
How long does the integration take?
Most standard integrations are done in 5–7 business days. Simple HTML sites take 3–4 days. Complex or multi-outlet projects may take 2–3 weeks. Your project manager will give an exact timeline after reviewing your site.
Will it change my site's design?
No. We match your existing design — colours, fonts, layout. The voice ordering button and billing panels blend into your site. We can also deliver features as a separate admin panel if you prefer the customer-facing site unchanged.
What if I need changes after delivery?
You get 30 days of free bug-fix support and 90 days of email support after delivery. For new features after that, we can quote per-project or offer a maintenance retainer. Since you own the code, any developer can also make changes.
Do I need to provide server access?
For platforms like WordPress or Webflow, we just need editor or admin access. For custom-built sites, temporary SSH or FTP access is needed during integration. All access is revoked once the project is complete.
Can I switch to the monthly plan later?
Yes. If you later want automatic updates and managed hosting through VoiceBill, you can switch to Pay As You Go at any time. Your integration work will be migrated at no extra cost.
