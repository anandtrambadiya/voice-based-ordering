FAQ

Common questions

Do I own the website and source code after launch?
Yes — 100%. Once the project is complete and final payment is made, you receive the full source code, design files, and all assets. There are no licensing fees or lock-in. You are free to host it anywhere or have any developer maintain it.
How long does it take to build the website?
Typically 4–6 weeks depending on the package and complexity. Starter projects often complete in 3 weeks. Enterprise projects with custom features may take up to 8 weeks. We provide a clear timeline at the start of every project.
What do I need to provide to get started?
Your menu (we can digitise a physical one), your logo (or we can suggest a designer), and any photos of your restaurant or food. Everything else — hosting, domain, design, development, and VoiceBill setup — is handled by our team.
Is hosting and domain included?
Starter includes 6 months of free hosting and SSL. Professional and Enterprise include 1–2 years. Domain registration (yourrestaurant.com) is included in Professional and Enterprise, or can be added to Starter at cost. After the included period, renewal is approximately ₹4,999/year for hosting.
Are there any monthly fees after launch?
No mandatory monthly fees during the included hosting period. After that, you can choose our optional maintenance plan at ₹499/month (hosting + security updates + minor edits), or host and maintain it yourself — we'll give you full documentation.
Can I add extra pages or features later?
Absolutely. Additional pages and features can be quoted and added at any time. Since you own the source code, you can also have your own developer add features independently.
Does it support multiple restaurant branches?
Yes — the Enterprise plan includes multi-branch support with separate menus, billing, and staff management per location, all under one owner dashboard. For Starter and Professional, multi-branch can be added as a custom feature.
