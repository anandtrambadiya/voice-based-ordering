FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?
No credit card required. You get 14 days of full platform access completely free. We'll only ask for payment details if you decide to continue after the trial.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Cancel from your dashboard at any time with one click. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period — no pro-rata charges.
Does it work on mobile and tablet?
Yes. VoiceBill is fully responsive and works on any browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge. The voice ordering works best on Chrome and Chrome for Android.
How many staff accounts do I get?
The Pay As You Go plan includes up to 5 staff accounts with role-based access — admin, waiter, and kitchen roles. Need more? Contact us for a custom quote.
Is my restaurant data safe?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We perform daily backups and maintain a 99.9% uptime SLA. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.
What languages does voice ordering support?
Currently English (Indian English) and Hindi are fully supported with 99.8% accuracy. Support for Gujarati, Marathi, Tamil, and Telugu is on our roadmap for Q3 2026.
